Privacy Policy

Salah Habit · Last updated: July 8, 2026

Salah Habit ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses, how it's used, and your choices. Salah Habit is built to work almost entirely on your device — we do not operate our own backend servers that store your personal data.

Information We Use

  • Location. With your permission, Salah Habit uses your device's location to calculate accurate prayer times for your area. This is processed on your device and is never transmitted to us or stored on any server we control.
  • Prayer & app activity. Your prayer completion history, streaks, and app preferences (such as your name and notification settings) are stored locally on your device and are not sent to us.
  • Notifications. Prayer time reminders and other in-app notifications are scheduled directly on your device and are never routed through an external server.
  • Purchases & subscriptions. If you subscribe to Focus Mode Pro, your purchase is processed by Apple, and subscription status is verified using RevenueCat, our subscription management provider. RevenueCat may receive a device identifier and purchase/transaction data necessary to validate your entitlement. See RevenueCat's privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.
  • Screen Time / App Blocking. If you enable Focus Mode, app selections you make are handled by Apple's Screen Time (Family Controls) framework directly on your device. We do not receive or see which apps you've chosen to block.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not use advertising or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not require an account or collect your email address to use the app.

Third-Party Services

Salah Habit relies on the following third parties, solely for the purposes described above:

  • Apple (App Store, StoreKit, Screen Time)
  • RevenueCat (subscription management)

Children's Privacy

Salah Habit is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@salahhabit.app.
