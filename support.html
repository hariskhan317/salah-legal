Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enable prayer time notifications?

Go to Settings in the app and turn on Notifications. You'll be asked to grant permission once — after that, reminders are scheduled automatically for each prayer.

How does Focus Mode work?

Focus Mode blocks distracting apps you choose during a prayer window, and unlocks them once you've marked that prayer as done. You'll need to select which apps to block using Apple's Screen Time picker, available under Settings → Focus Mode.

How do I restore my Focus Mode Pro purchase?

Open the paywall screen (tap the Focus Mode upsell card on the home screen, or the toggle in Settings) and tap "Restore purchase." Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used to originally subscribe.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple, not within the app. On your iPhone, go to Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → Salah Habit, and cancel from there.

My prayer times look off — how can I fix that?

Prayer times are calculated from your device's location. Make sure Location Services are enabled for Salah Habit under your device's Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services.
